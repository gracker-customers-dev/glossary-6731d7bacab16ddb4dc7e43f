Globalplatform

A specification and standardization body for secure application management on various devices.

Description

GlobalPlatform is an international organization that provides specifications for the secure management of applications on secure chip technology. It aims to promote the standardization of secure application management across various devices and platforms, including smart cards, mobile devices, and other embedded systems. GlobalPlatform defines the architecture and protocols needed to ensure that applications can be securely loaded, managed, and removed from secure elements. This is particularly important in the context of identity and access management (IAM) as it ensures that sensitive information is protected and managed appropriately. The organization facilitates interoperability between different technology providers and ensures that secure applications can operate across different devices and environments without compatibility issues. By establishing these standards, GlobalPlatform plays a crucial role in enabling secure digital transactions, protecting user identities, and facilitating trust in electronic services.

Examples

  • Secure payment applications on mobile devices using GlobalPlatform standards.
  • Digital identity management solutions that leverage GlobalPlatform specifications.

Additional Information

  • GlobalPlatform works with various stakeholders, including technology providers, service providers, and government bodies.
  • The organization also offers certification programs to ensure compliance with its specifications.
