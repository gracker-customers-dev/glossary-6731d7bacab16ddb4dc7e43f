Appraisal team member (ATM)

An individual who is part of a team responsible for assessing and evaluating the performance and effectiveness of an organization's information asset management.

Description

An Appraisal Team Member (ATM) plays a crucial role in the context of Information Asset Management (IAM) by participating in the systematic evaluation of an organization's information assets. This may include data, documents, systems, and processes that are critical to the organization's operations. ATMs are typically involved in conducting assessments to determine the value, risks, and compliance of these assets. They collaborate with other team members to gather information, analyze data, and provide insights that inform decision-making related to the management and governance of information assets. The contribution of ATMs is essential in ensuring that the organization effectively manages its information resources, aligns them with strategic objectives, and adheres to relevant regulations and standards. Their expertise may vary across disciplines, bringing diverse perspectives to the appraisal process, which enhances the overall effectiveness of IAM initiatives.

Examples

  • A data analyst evaluating the effectiveness of a data management system during an IAM audit.
  • A compliance officer assessing the organization's adherence to regulatory requirements concerning information retention and access.

Additional Information

  • ATMs may have backgrounds in data governance, compliance, IT management, or risk assessment.
  • Effective communication and teamwork skills are essential for ATMs to collaborate with various stakeholders.
