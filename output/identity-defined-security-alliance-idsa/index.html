Identity Defined Security Alliance (IDSA)

A collaborative initiative focused on integrating identity management and security to enhance organizational security posture.

Description

The Identity Defined Security Alliance (IDSA) is a consortium of identity and security industry leaders dedicated to promoting the integration of identity management with security practices. The alliance aims to foster collaboration among organizations to improve their cybersecurity frameworks, particularly by leveraging identity as a security perimeter. In today's digital landscape, where cyber threats are increasingly sophisticated, IDSA emphasizes the importance of securing identities as a foundational element of an organization's security strategy. The alliance provides resources, best practices, and frameworks that help organizations align their identity management processes with broader security strategies, ensuring that access controls are effectively enforced and monitored. By advocating for the adoption of identity-centric security measures, IDSA seeks to help organizations mitigate risks associated with data breaches, unauthorized access, and other identity-related security threats.

Examples

  • Organizations implementing zero trust security models that leverage identity.
  • Companies using IDSA guidelines to enhance their identity governance and administration.

Additional Information

  • IDSA promotes collaboration among technology vendors, practitioners, and end-users.
  • The alliance provides a framework for integrating identity management with risk management and compliance.
